NuVu Meter

2024-02-29
#iot  #electronics 
I had a few salvaged VU meters burning a hole in my proverbial pocket, so I decided to turn one into a readout for my home’s energy usage. At a high level, I have an Emporia Vue 2 that publishes energy readings to a self-hosted MQTT server (this is not default behavior and required flashing custom config to the device). My self-hosted NodeRed instance listens for those messages, extracts the relevant data, and issues a new message with the total point-in-time energy usage as a percentage of the last day’s max usage (it also saves all of the data so I can view it in Grafana).
